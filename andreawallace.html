SENIOR VP
+15 years as a public relations, marketing, & crisis communications professional

Award-winning marketing communications professional with 10+ years of consumer focused marketing, public relations and broadcast media background on both the corporate and agency side. Consumer industry focus includes healthcare, wellness, real estate, CPG, retail, non-profit, auto and luxury brands. Andrea’s passion includes working with small to medium sized businesses or agencies to provide marketing and PR communications management support.

Andrea Wallace has worked at Cohn & Wolfe as a Vice President leading top healthcare accounts and national PR campaigns. Andrea has served as an account leader in several other agencies including Ketchum and Porter Novelli, where she serviced luxury brands and healthcare clients as well as Steel Branding, a boutique agency focused on family branding, and Screamer Co. Prior to agency life, Andrea was the spokeswoman for Gwinnett Medical Center, a not-for-profit healthcare network, where she managed external relations, crisis communications, produced a local TV program, planned community outreach and managed all media relations resulting in programs still running today and successfully placing the most earned media in the tenure of the position in a top 5 media DMA.

Andrea also started the AMA’s first Healthcare SIG which targets marketers and communicators in the healthcare industry bringing like-minded professionals together for growth and development.
