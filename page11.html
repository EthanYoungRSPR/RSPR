Stifel Fixed Income Case Study

Objective

To raise Stifel's Chief Economist Lindsey Piegza’s visibility and credibility with clients, stakeholders and the investment community at large – institutional and retail.

Methods

No two clients are alike, so we customize each communication strategy to fit the needs of our individual clients. This campaign included the following tactics, among others: 

High-Profile Placements

Deliver high-profile placements by leveraging existing relationships with producers to continually place Lindsey Piegza on financial TV, radio, print, and digital outlets

Creative Story Synthesis

Monitor financial news trends and utilize our experience with developing unique stories to develop pitch angles.

Thought Leadership

Work with lindsey to identify issues and create strategic plans to build and maintain relationships with key publics, specifically using Lindsey’s economic notes

Results

• Average of 150 media appearances monthly in premier financial publications (print + online)
• Average of 10 TV appearances monthly on CNBC, Bloomberg Television, Fox Business, BNN, CNBC
• TV viewers more than 250,000 a day
• Print circulation more than 1 million a day
