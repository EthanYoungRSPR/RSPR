First Franklin Case Study

Objective

To position and establish Brett Ewing and First Franklin as thought leaders in wealth management strategy.
And to create an affinity toward First Franklin among potential clients in Florida.

Methods

No two clients are alike, so we customize each communication strategy to fit the needs of our individual clients. This campaign included the following tactics, among others: 

High-Profile Placements

Deliver high-profile placements by leveraging existing relationships with producers to secure regular interviews for Brett on financial TV, radio, print and online

Creative Story Synthesis

Constantly monitor financial news trends to develop ongoing pitch angles

Thought Leadership

Work with First Franklin to identify issues and create strategic plans to build and maintain relationships with key publics, specifically by placing timely articles by Brett into key wealth management publications and using his market commentary notes to obtain media placements.

Media Tours

We connected First Franklin to key members of the media and helped them build lasting relationships, specifically through media tours and meet and greet opportunities
