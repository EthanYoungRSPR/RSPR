Live and Invest Overseas [LIOS] Case Study

Objective

Founded in 2008, Live and Invest Overseas was looking to expand their visibility with audiences in real estate investment, travel, living and retirement overseas. Also to provide continuous, highly visible media interviews and story placements that build the LIOS brand, improve visibility and sales.

Methods

No two clients are alike, so we customize each communication strategy to fit the needs of our individual clients. This campaign included the following tactics, among others: 

High-Profile Placements

Deliver high-profile placements by leveraging existing relationships with producers to continually place LIOS in print and digital outlets.

Creative Story Synthesis

Constantly monitor of real estate, travel and retirement news trends to develop angles to pitch Kathleen Peddicord and Lief Simon to reporters and producers.

Thought Leadership

Work with LIOS to identify issues and create strategic plans to build and maintain relationships with key publics, specifically by analyzing and distributing relevant blog posts to reporters and producers to obtain interview opportunities.

Media Tours

We connected LIOS to key social media influencers and helped them build lasting relationships, specifically through media tours and meet and greet opportunities for Kathleen and Lief. This allowed them to meet with the press on-on-one, especially as they travel.

Results

• Average of 75 media appearances monthly in national and local television, radio, wires, print–including daily papers, business journals and trade outlets–and digital publications.
• Prior to utilizing Razor Sharp PR’s services, LIOS was only getting media interviews periodically.
